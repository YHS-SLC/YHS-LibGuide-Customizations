Struggling? Need Support?

YOU ARE NOT ALONE

If you or someone you know is struggling with their mental health, support is available. You are not a burden and you are not alone. You may contact your YHS Personal Counsellor, seek external community supports, or private supports.

YHS Personal Counsellors

  • Located in the underground across from the Grad Lounge
  • Available during school hours
  • Drop-in, or email to book

Community Supports

  • CYMH (VCH) clinics offer counselling and psychiatry support for 19 years and under: call 604-331-8908 to book
  • Youth Clinics of sexual, physical, and mental health support for 24 years and under. Visit www.vch.ca/youthclinic to find a clinic near you
  • The Foundry provides virtual or in-person counselling for 24 years and under at www.foundrybc.ca
  • Private Therapists can be found through the BCACC or the BCPA websites

Crisis Supports

  • Crisis Line: 604-872-3311
  • Kids Help Phone: 1-800-668-6868 or text CONNECT to 686868
  • Hotline: 1-800-784-2433
  • Online chats at youthinbc.com or www.CrisisCentreChat.ca

Or call 9-1-1 and request "mental health support"

Or go to your nearest emergency room

 

 

Foundry

Foundry

"Having support from someone who understood me and how hard it was to manage my mental health was so helpful. I went from feeling alone and hiding to coming out and feeling connected. I am so grateful." - Youth Client

Ways to Access Virtual Services:

  • Drop-in services
  • Scheduled appointments
  • Group sessions

Video, audio and chat options available

Hours: 7 days/week 1:00pm-9:00pm
Drop-in Services: Tuesday-Thursday 2:00pm-6:00pm

Download the Foundry BC app

Foundry Virutal BC offers free counselling, peer support, substance use services, groups, primary care and employment services to young people ages 12-24 and their caregivers across British Columbia.

Don't have a local Foundry Centre in your community? Foundry Virtual BC services do not require a referral, and are free, safe, and confidential.

Download the app on the App Store, Google Play, or access it in a browser.

To learn more, please call 1-833-308-6379, email online@foundrybc.ca or visit www.foundrybc.ca/virtual
